All About Us

We are a simple team of three CS students working to make a Secure Online Payment System, called Easy Pay!


We are working to practice securing sample user information with a simple demo website that will simulate an online payment system. Users will be able to do the following:


  • Create an Account that will be stored on a MySQL database
  • Login at anytime with a secure password as well as the usage of MFA
  • Add/Remove money to their Easy Pay account
  • Send "money" to other users in our system
  • Change account information
